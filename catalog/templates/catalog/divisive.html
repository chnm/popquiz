How It Works

The Divisiveness Score measures how many people are on the minority side of the vote. Higher scores mean more people on BOTH sides!

  • Score = min(yes votes, no votes) - the number of people on the smaller side
  • Only movies with 5+ total votes are included
  • A movie with 10 yes and 10 no votes is more divisive than 10 yes and 1 no
  • These are the movies that spark the most debate!
