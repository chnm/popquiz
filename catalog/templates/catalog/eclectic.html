How It Works

The Eclectic Score measures how often someone's rating differs from the team consensus. A higher score means more unique taste!

  • For each movie, the consensus is the most common rating (loved/liked/okay/disliked/hated)
  • Movies with tied ratings or only one rating are excluded
  • Users need at least 3 comparable ratings to appear on this list
  • "Contrarian picks" shows movies where this person rated against the majority
